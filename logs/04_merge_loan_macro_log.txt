Methodological Note
----------------------------------------------------------------------
The macro variables were merged by origination year. This is appropriate for contextual portfolio-risk analysis because the supplied loan dataset is loan-level origination data rather than a full monthly balance-sheet time series. The merged macro variables should not be interpreted as causal drivers without more granular borrower-level and time-series data.
