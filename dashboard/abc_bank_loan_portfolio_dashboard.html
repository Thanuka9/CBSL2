5. Underlying Drivers of Risk

Model interpretation: The Random Forest risk-driver model achieved ROC AUC of 0.8737. The strongest model driver is rating_rank, showing that credit rating quality is the dominant available signal for identifying non-performing loans in the supplied dataset. This is an explanatory screening model, not a production regulatory PD model.
